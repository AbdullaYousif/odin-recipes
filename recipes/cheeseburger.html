Cheeseburger

Description

A cheeseburger is a classic sandwich made of a juicy beef patty topped with melted cheese, nestled between a soft bun, and often adorned with lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, and condiments for a deliciously satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80/20 blend for best flavor)
  • Cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • Burger buns (soft, toasted)
  • Lettuce (leaf or shredded)
  • Tomato (sliced)
  • Pickles (slices)
  • Onion (raw, grilled, or caramelized)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt and pepper (for seasoning)

Steps

  1. Form Patties: Shape ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook Patties: Grill or pan-fry the patties for 3-4 minutes per side, adding cheese during the last minute.
  3. Toast Buns: Toast the burger buns until golden.
  4. Assemble: Place the patty on the bottom bun and add lettuce, tomato, pickles, and onion.
  5. Top and Serve: Add ketchup and mustard on the top bun, then close the burger and serve.
